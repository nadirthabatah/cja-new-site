Meet Sensei Carrie Chandler

Owner and Head Instructor Carrie Chandler is a National Team Coach for USA Judo and was formerly the U.S. #1 ranked athlete for many years. As a competitor Carrie trained under 4x Olympian Jason Morris in upstate New York.

Carrie has been an integral part in running some of the biggest Judo programs in the United States, including at the United States Olympic Training Center in Colorado Springs.

Sensei Carrie is a 5x U.S. National Champion, Pan American Champion, multiple time World Team member and international medalist. Carrie reached a career high of being ranked #9 on the Senior World ranking list.
